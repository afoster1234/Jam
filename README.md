# Mid-Semester Jam!
### By: Austin Foster
Play Here: https://afoster1234.github.io/Jam/

## Controls
* A: Move Left
* D: Move Right
* Spcae Bar: Jump

### INSTRUCTIONS
* Find the pumpkins! There are 5 of them. 
* Once you get them all, travel through the door at the top of the haunted castle to escape and win the game!
